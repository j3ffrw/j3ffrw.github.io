How to use Chipkit’s Max32 as USB Adapter for Xbee radios

Here’s an alternative way of hooking up your xbee to your computer using your existing Max32 or any 3.3V arduino-compatible boards.

Xbee   to   Max32 
DOUT ------ 1(TX0)
DIN  ------ 0(RX0)
VCC  ------ 3.3V
GND  ------ GND
RST to GND ( This is to deactivate the max32 mcu)
